What the site does

A planning tool for cat shelves, bridges, hideouts, and climbing routes.

Design The Space

Set wall dimensions, add corners, choose wall colors and materials, then place pieces on a scaled wall.

Preview In 2D And 3D

Switch between a clean layout view and a 3D room preview to judge spacing, depth, and visual balance.

Price The Build

Add catalog items, estimate hardware, track total cost, and compare the plan against your target budget.

Import Product URLs

Look up accessible product pages to fill price, rating, dimensions, and product images for generated models.

Size Around Your Cat

Adjust cat length, height, and weight so the route is checked against reach, landing room, and load assumptions.

Review The Plan

Get a summary of pieces, route measurements, warnings, and a shopping-style list before you install anything.
